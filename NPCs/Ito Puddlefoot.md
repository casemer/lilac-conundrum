Half-Orc woman, wife and mother who was apart of a scam [[Clarence Providence]] ran in the square. 

[[Mr. Frank Puddlefoot]] is her husband and while talking to the party, mentioned that Ito was sneaking out at night but never knew what she was up to.

She was near death when the party found her. Luckily she was brought to her home in the 6th precinct where she should make a full recovery.
