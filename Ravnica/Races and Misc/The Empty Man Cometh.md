A childhood scary story that [[Babolax (Babo) Boffins|Babo]] is aware of. He forgot about it until he saw the man at the stop of the stairs.

The empty man is an idea of the impending doom of civilization. Its the fear that you have in the back of your mind and that you can't do anything about it. Letting this fear overtake you into insanity. Its about how to allow those overtake your fear and how it relates to psychedelics.
