The group gathers at a crypt in a square, where they meet [[John Patsalos]], an ex-lawyer, who is investigating [[Brondov Cafferes]], a senator suspected of taking bribes and murder. [[Father Reymani]], a seedy priest, arrives first, followed by [[Clive Burnstem]], an activist and detective, and [[Wallace Moist]], a librarian linked to [[House Dimir]]. [[Babolax (Babo) Boffins|Babo Boffins]], a goblin toy designer recently fired for defective products (not his fault, he claims), arrives late, and [[Dante Allegory]], a college student from another realm, appears confused about his surroundings, feeling a calling to this location..

At [[Midnight]], they receive an assignment: [[Clarence Providence]], an [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]] priest and friend of Reymani, has accused a half-orc woman, [[Ito Puddlefoot]], of demonism after performing a public exorcism on her. The group heads to investigate. Babo learns the next event will take place tomorrow at noon in Brondov’s courtroom. Wallace’s book shows a dwarven figure, which Babo recognizes as [[Thiggis Metalmail]], a dwarf who hired him for a job involving demon blood.

They find Ito unconscious and severely injured in a gutter. Clive provides water, while Reymani suggests prayer. She was still possessed but they didn't use holy water which means the demon was weak to ammonia instead of holy water. After confirming her condition is due to chemical burns (from ammonia), they transport her to her home, where her husband reveals she has been leaving late at night for the past month but won’t explain where.

The group is directed to focus on Brondov Cafferes’ court session tomorrow. A newspaper name [[The Gazette]] headline links Cafferes, Thiggis, and [[Lilie Halview]] (a Boros Legion officer involved in a beast incident near Clive’s home) to possible demon possession. They realize these individuals are connected to the mystery they’re investigating.
